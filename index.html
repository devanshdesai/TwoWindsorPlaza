About Two Windsor Plaza

Centrally located between New York City, Philadelphia, and New Jersey, Two Windsor Plaza is a premier Class A office building in the heart of Allentown's industrial space. Two Windsor Plaza offers easy access to all major air, rail, and roadways as the Pennsylvania Turnpike, Interstate 78, and the Lehigh Valley International Airport are all conveiniently close to the attractive 10 acre site.

The sleek architecture design of the building reflects the cutting-edge technology that is evident throughout the facility. From the advanced climate control system to the ultra-fast fiber optic internet, Two Windsor Plaza is the pinnacle of comfort, technology, and efficiency. The modular layout grants you the flexibility to design your space that meets, and exceeds, your individual requirements.
